## Stainless Steel Nitrogen Flushing & Sealing Machine - Product Overview
Elevate your food packaging standards with our **Stainless Steel Nitrogen Flushing Machine**. This 2-in-1 system is designed specifically for the Sri Lankan food industry, allowing manufacturers to flush high-purity nitrogen into pouches immediately before sealing. 

By replacing oxygen with nitrogen, you prevent oxidation (rancidity) and create a protective "air cushion" that prevents delicate items like chips and crackers from breaking during transit. This full Stainless Steel (Grade 304) model ensures the highest hygienic standards for food production.
