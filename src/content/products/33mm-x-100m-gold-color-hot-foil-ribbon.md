### Expert Setup for Metallic Foils
To achieve the perfect "Mirror Gold" effect on your **Date Coding Machine**:
1. **Pre-Heating:** Ensure your machine has reached a stable temperature. Gold foils typically require a slightly higher dwell time or temperature than standard black ribbons.
2. **Smooth Pressure:** Use a firm, consistent stamping pressure to ensure the metallic pigment transfers solidly without "pinholing."
3. **Storage Care:** To maintain the brilliant metallic luster, store rolls in their original packaging away from direct sunlight and high humidity.
