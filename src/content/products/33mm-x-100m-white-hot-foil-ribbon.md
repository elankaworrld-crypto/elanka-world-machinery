### Application Tips for White Foil
To get the best results with white ribbon on your **Date Coding Machine**:
1. **Temperature:** White pigments often require a slightly higher temperature (approx. +10°C) than black ribbons to achieve maximum opacity.
2. **Pressure:** Ensure even pressure across the stamping head for a solid, non-transparent white finish.
3. **Storage:** Keep rolls in a cool, dry place to maintain the integrity of the white thermal coating.
